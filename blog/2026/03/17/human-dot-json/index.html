    Tue Mar 17 2026 23:22:00 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time) - by Astra

    I was scrolling Bluesky when I came across a post that had been reposted by @soatok.bsky.social about something called "human.json". The human.json protocol description calls out that a ton of internet content today is AI generated slop and posits that we need a way to declare that you are human on the web.

    As someone who is getting more and more tired of the slop filled world we live in, I thought this would make a great addition to my site. /human.json serves to verify that I am a living creature posting here on the net, and not a large language model. I will, as needed, update my vouches list to include sites of folks I know are not robots (unless their sona is a robot, but y'know what I mean).

    I highly recommend checking out the project and think this would be a neat addition to anyone's personal site.

    Thank you Soatok for reposting that post that led me to this project (and in general for all of your posts! not that you'll read this!)

    As a side note, the README for human.json calls out the /AI slashpage, linking to https://slashpages.net/. Now to explore what other slash-pages I can add to my site...
